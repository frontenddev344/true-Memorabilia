Returns Policy

At true-Memorabilia, we take pride in offering 100% authentic signed football jerseys that are carefully inspected, verified, and securely shipped. Please review our returns policy below before placing an order.

Authenticity Guarantee

All items sold on true-Memorabilia are independently authenticated and guaranteed to be genuine. Because of the nature of signed memorabilia, we do not accept returns based on buyer’s remorse or change of mind.

Eligible Returns

We accept returns only under the following circumstances:

  • The item received is not as described
  • The item arrives damaged during shipping
  • The item is proven to be inauthentic (subject to verification)

Any issues must be reported to us within 48 hours of delivery.

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return:

  • The item must be in the same condition as received
  • Authentication tags, holograms, and certificates must remain intact and unaltered
  • The item must not have been worn, washed, framed, or modified

Items that do not meet these conditions will not be eligible for a refund.

Return Process

Contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order

Provide your order number and clear photographs of the issue

Once approved, we will provide return instructions

Returns sent without prior approval will not be accepted.

Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed once the item has been received and inspected. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the buyer unless the item is faulty, damaged, or incorrectly described

International Orders

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees. These charges are non-refundable.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our returns policy or a specific order, please contact us — we’re always happy to help.
